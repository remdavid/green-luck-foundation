More About Green Luck Foundation

The Green Luck Foundation is a non-governmental organization dedicated to promoting agricultural sustainability and environmental conservation. Founded by Keziah Wendy Aloo, the organization is supported by executive members Florence Auma and Paul Omollo. The foundation's primary mission is to enhance food security and resilience in communities by focusing on the cultivation of drought-resistant crops.

This initiative is particularly crucial in regions affected by climate change, where traditional farming methods may no longer be viable. By promoting the growth of these resilient crops, the Green Luck Foundation aims to ensure that communities can thrive even in challenging environmental conditions. In addition to agricultural initiatives, the foundation is actively involved in tree planting efforts. Trees play a vital role in combating climate change by absorbing carbon dioxide and providing essential habitats for wildlife. The Green Luck Foundation encourages community participation in these tree-planting activities, fostering a sense of stewardship for the environment.

Our Vision

Founded with a vision to nurture resilient communities and foster hope for the future, the Green Luck Foundation is a non-profit nongovernmental organization dedicated to agricultural sustainability and climate action.

A Mission Rooted in Sustainability

At the heart of the Green Luck Foundation lies a commitment to empowering communities by promoting the cultivation of drought-resistant crops. In a world increasingly impacted by climate change, these efforts ensure food security even in the harshest conditions, enabling farmers to thrive against the odds.

Meaningful Actions for Lasting Impact

Our work extends beyond farming: planting trees to restore balance in our ecosystems, digging trenches to aid water retention, and implementing community projects that combat the devastating effects of climate change. Each initiative is designed to reinforce the environment and uplift the lives that depend on it.

Growing Together in Faith and Knowledge

Guided by the foundational leadership of co-founder Keziah Wendy Aloo and spearheaded by executive members Florence Auma and Paul Omollo, Green Luck Foundation also shares the gospel of God. We believe that inspiring spiritual growth alongside environmental awareness creates a powerful foundation for lasting change. Our commitment includes educating communities about climate change, fostering a shared sense of responsibility for our planet.

A Greener Tomorrow

The Green Luck Foundation invites everyone to join in reclaiming a healthier planet. By blending innovation, faith, and community spirit, we are planting the seeds for a future where nature and humanity flourish in harmony.
